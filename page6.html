WHO WE ARE

Nueva Ecija Backpackers & Mountaineering Community (NEBMC) was founded on April 22, 2015 and registered as Nueva Ecija Backpackers & Mountaineering Community, Inc. (NEMBCI) in Security of Exchange Commission (SEC) on March 07, 2016.
NEBMCI is an ECO-FRIENDLY and WILDLIFE EXPLORATION organization that aims to encourage a green and healthy environment. The Organization’s main objective is to encourage the conservation of our natural scenery, to foster hiking, trail construction and maintenance, fellowship, and enjoyment of the out-of-doors in Organization’s members, and to promote the majestic yet unexplored beauty of the province through ecotourism while protecting the same from any form of acts that threatens their natural wonders.
It aims to lead the basic of outdoor and recreational consciousness. It is engaged in various outdoor activities such as tree planting, feeding program, gift giving, medical mission, mountain climbing, water tubing, spelunking, snorkeling, rappelling, kayaking, and others.

MISSION

To encourage our fellow Novo Ecijanos to a green and healthy environment by conserving our natural scenery, to foster hiking, trail construction and maintenance, fellowship, and enjoyment of the great out-of-doors.

VISION

To create an evironment-friendly community of peace loving and responsible citizens who are very much concern in protecting our environment from human threats.
